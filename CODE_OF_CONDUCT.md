# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We want Caelestia-NixOS to be a welcoming project for everyone.

Regardless of experience level, background or operating system, every respectful contribution is appreciated.

Learning is one of the main goals of this project.

Questions are welcome.

Mistakes are expected.

Curiosity is encouraged.

---

## Our Standards

Examples of behaviour that help create a positive environment include:

- Being respectful.
- Helping newcomers.
- Sharing knowledge.
- Explaining decisions.
- Giving constructive feedback.
- Being patient.
- Celebrating learning.

---

## Unacceptable Behaviour

The following behaviour is not welcome:

- Personal attacks.
- Harassment.
- Discrimination.
- Insults.
- Gatekeeping.
- Discouraging beginners.

This project exists to make Linux and NixOS more approachable, not less.

---

## Our Philosophy

Everyone starts somewhere.

Nobody knows everything.

Helping someone learn today creates tomorrow's contributor.

Kindness costs nothing.

---

Thank you for helping make Caelestia-NixOS a friendly place to learn and build.
