# Frequently Asked Questions

## Why NixOS?

Because it provides reproducible systems.

---

## Why Home Manager?

To manage user configuration declaratively.

---

## Why Hyprland?

Because it offers a modern Wayland compositor with excellent performance.

---

## Why UWSM?

It provides a clean and reliable graphical session.

---

## Why Caelestia Shell?

It offers a beautiful and highly customizable desktop shell while integrating well with Hyprland.

---

## Why not modify upstream projects?

Because using official modules makes maintenance significantly easier.

---

## Why does the repository resemble ~/.config?

To make the project feel familiar to users coming from traditional Linux distributions.

---

## I'm new to NixOS. Can I still use this project?

Absolutely.

The documentation was written specifically to make learning easier.
