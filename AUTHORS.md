## AI Collaboration (ChatGPT)

Caelestia-NixOS would not exist in its current form without ChatGPT.

Artificial intelligence was not used to replace engineering decisions or learning.

Instead, it became a technical partner throughout the project, helping to:

- discuss architecture
- investigate bugs
- review documentation
- improve repository organization
- challenge design decisions
- explain complex NixOS concepts
- accelerate learning

Every important technical decision was discussed, tested and ultimately validated by the project maintainer.

The goal was never to let AI build the project.

The goal was to build the project **with AI while understanding every important decision.**

---

Caelestia-NixOS was built through collaboration, curiosity and a shared commitment to understanding every technical decision.

This project demonstrates how human curiosity and artificial intelligence can complement each other to build better software and better documentation.
